ROSENET

This is a published research paper introducing RoseNet, a deep learning model for predicting energy metrics for double InDel mutants. Double InDel mutants are protein mutations generated by inserting or deleting two amino acids from a protein.

This paper was published as part of CSBW (the Computational Structural Biology Workshop) in the 14th ACM International Conference on Bioinformatics, Computational Biology, and Health Informatics (ACM-BCB) in September 2023.



The paper can be found here on ACMs digital library, or read for free here on arXiv.
